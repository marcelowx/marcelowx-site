About Me / Sobre Mí

English

I am Marcelo Rivera González, a bilingual meteorologist and science communicator. My work focuses on clear, useful, and responsible weather information for communities across the Rio Grande Valley, South Texas, Puerto Rico, and audiences interested in tropical meteorology.

My academic background includes a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, a Master’s degree in Labor and Industrial Relations, and a Bachelor’s degree in Meteorology. I am currently pursuing a second Master’s degree in Geography, Climate Science, and Geospatial Science.

Before becoming a meteorologist, I worked for nearly a decade in higher education administration, serving as a University Registrar for universities in Puerto Rico and Texas. That experience shaped how I communicate today: with service, clarity, education, and community impact at the center.

I later worked as a meteorologist and climate change reporter for NBCUniversal in California. Today, I serve as a meteorologist for NBC23 and CBS4 in the Rio Grande Valley, Texas, where I continue my mission of helping people understand the weather and make informed decisions before, during, and after impactful weather events.

Español

Soy Marcelo Rivera González, meteorólogo bilingüe y comunicador científico. Mi trabajo se enfoca en ofrecer información del tiempo clara, útil y responsable para comunidades del Valle del Río Grande, el sur de Texas, Puerto Rico y audiencias interesadas en la meteorología tropical.

Mi preparación académica incluye un Bachillerato (licenciatura) en Administración de Empresas, una Maestría en Relaciones Laborales e Industriales y un Bachillerato (licenciatura) en Meteorología. Actualmente curso una segunda maestría en Geografía, Ciencias del Clima y Ciencia Geoespacial.

Antes de convertirme en meteorólogo, trabajé por casi una década en la administración universitaria, sirviendo como Registrador Universitario en instituciones de educación superior en Puerto Rico y Texas. Esa experiencia moldeó la forma en que comunico hoy: con servicio, claridad, educación e impacto comunitario como prioridad.

Más adelante, trabajé como meteorólogo y reportero de cambio climático para NBCUniversal en California. Actualmente, trabajo como meteorólogo para NBC23 y CBS4 en el Valle del Río Grande, Texas, donde continúo mi misión de ayudar a las personas a entender el tiempo y tomar decisiones informadas antes, durante y después de eventos meteorológicos de impacto.

RGV Weather Focus / Enfoque del Tiempo en el Valle

Weather in the Rio Grande Valley can change fast, from extreme heat and humidity to sea breeze storms, tropical moisture, coastal flooding, and hurricane season impacts. This section highlights the local weather themes I track closely.

🌡️ Heat & Humidity

Updates focused on heat index, hydration, outdoor safety, and the local impacts of humidity across McAllen, Harlingen, Brownsville, Weslaco, Starr County, and the coast.

⛈️ Storms & Flooding

Clear explanations of thunderstorms, heavy rain, isolated flooding, severe weather threats, and why not every storm affects every city the same way.

🌊 Coastal Weather

Forecast context for South Padre Island, coastal winds, rip currents, beach plans, sea breeze showers, and tropical moisture moving into deep South Texas.

🌀 Tropical Awareness

Hurricane science, tropical outlooks, preparedness messaging, and practical explanations without hype or panic.
